## Statement (Silverman V.1 setup)
For an elliptic curve `E/F_q` with q-power Frobenius `π`,
`E(F_q) = ker(1 − π : E → E)`.

That is, a point `P ∈ E(K̄)` is `F_q`-rational iff `π(P) = P`, iff
`(1 − π)(P) = O`.
