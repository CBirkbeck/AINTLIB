## Statement (Silverman II.4)
For a smooth curve `C/K`, the **space of (meromorphic) differential forms** on `C`,
denoted `Ω_C`, is the `K(C)`-vector space generated by symbols `df` for
`f ∈ K(C)`, modulo the relations
- `d(f + g) = df + dg`
- `d(fg) = f·dg + g·df`
- `dα = 0 for α ∈ K`.

This is the module of Kähler differentials of `K(C)/K`.
