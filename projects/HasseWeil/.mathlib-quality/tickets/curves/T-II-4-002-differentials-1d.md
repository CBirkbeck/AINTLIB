## Statement (Silverman II.4.2(a))
For a smooth curve `C/K`, the `K(C)`-vector space `Ω_C` is 1-dimensional.

## Acceptance criteria

```lean

/-- The differentials Ω_C form a 1-dimensional K(C)-vector space.
    Reference: Silverman II.4.2(a). -/
theorem Differentials.rank_one (C : SmoothPlaneCurve F) :
    Module.rank C.FunctionField (Differentials C) = 1

/-- Equivalent statement: Ω_C is finite-dimensional with finrank = 1. -/
theorem Differentials.finrank_one (C : SmoothPlaneCurve F) :
    Module.finrank C.FunctionField (Differentials C) = 1

end HasseWeil.Curves
```
