## Source proof (transcribed, NOT invented) — Wedhorn's actual route is ACYCLICITY

The Lean leaf packages *degree-0* of Wedhorn's full **`O_X`-acyclicity** argument. Wedhorn proves
`O_X` is a sheaf (Thm 8.28) for strongly-noetherian Tate `A` thus (wedhorn.txt:4214–4255):

1. **Prop A.4** (5332): a presheaf on a basis stable under finite intersection that is acyclic for
   every covering is a sheaf. So it suffices that *every open covering by rational subsets is
   `O_X`-acyclic*.
2. **Lemma 7.54** (3490, = "[Hu3] Lemma 2.6"): every open cover of `Spa A` refines to one of the
   form `U_t = R(T/t)` with `T ⊆ A` generating `A` as an ideal ("rational cover generated by `T`").
3. **Prop A.3(2)** (5320): acyclicity is invariant under refinement, so it suffices to prove
   acyclicity for rational covers generated by `T` — **Lemma 8.34**.
4. **Lemma 8.34** (4222): `A` complete strongly-noeth Tate, `U` rational cover generated by finite
   `T` with `T·A = A` ⟹ `U` is `O_X`-acyclic. Proof in 4 parts:
   - **(i)** For `f ∈ A`, the 2-set cover `U_f = {R(1/f), R(f/1)}` (= `{x(f)≤1}`, `{x(f)≥1}`) is
     acyclic by **Lemma 8.33**; restriction `U_f|U` to any rational `U` is acyclic; by **Prop
     A.3(3)** induction, **Laurent covers** `V = U_{f₁}×⋯×U_{fᵣ}` are acyclic, as are `V|U`.
   - **(ii)** For `T = (f₀,…,fₙ)` generating `A`, ∃ a Laurent cover `(Vⱼ)` with `U|Vⱼ` generated by
     **units** of `O_X(Vⱼ)`. Proof: ∀x ∃fᵢ with x(fᵢ)≠0; by **Cor 7.32** ∃ unit `s` with
     `x(s)<x(fᵢ)` ∀x; the Laurent cover gen by `s⁻¹f₁,…,s⁻¹fᵣ` works.
   - **(iii)** Every rational cover generated by units `f₀,…,fₙ` refines to the Laurent cover gen by
     `{fᵢfⱼ⁻¹}`.
   - **(iv)** Combine: (i)+(iii)+**A.3(2)** ⟹ restrictions of unit-generated covers acyclic; then
     for `U` gen by `T`, take `V` (by (ii)) with `U|V` unit-generated ⟹ `U|V` acyclic; `V|U` acyclic
     by (i); so `V` acyclic ⟹ `U` acyclic by **Prop A.3(1)**.
5. **Lemma 8.33** (4151): the 2-set augmented Čech complex `0 → O_X(X) → O_X(U₁)×O_X(U₂) →
   O_X(U₁∩U₂) → 0` is exact. Proof: `ε` injective (**Cor 8.32**); `O_X(U₁)=A⟨ζ⟩/(f−ζ)`,
   `O_X(U₂)=A⟨η⟩/(1−fη)`, `O_X(U₁∩U₂)=A⟨ζ,ζ⁻¹⟩/(f−ζ)` (**Examples 6.38/6.39**); a 3×3 commutative
   diagram with `ι, λ, λ′`, columns exact; the equations `A⟨ζ,ζ⁻¹⟩ = A⟨ζ⟩+ζ⁻¹A⟨ζ⁻¹⟩` give
   surjectivity of `λ,λ′` and `im(ι)=ker(λ)`; a diagram chase gives exactness of the bottom row.
